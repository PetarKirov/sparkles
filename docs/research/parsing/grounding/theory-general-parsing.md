## Discrepancies

No factual errors found in the page. Two soft caveats:

- ⚠ (date attribution, minor) — Kasami citation year. Page (L32, L111, L533) and Sources
  give **Kasami 1965**. The local textbook `grune-jacobs-ptapg-1st-ed-book.pdf` L4457 cites
  **Kasami [CF 1969]**, and the dragon book gives no explicit Kasami year in the body. 1965
  is the conventional date (AFCRL tech. report) and is defensible; flagged only because the
  one local artifact that dates Kasami disagrees. **No change recommended** unless the survey
  wants a footnote reconciling the 1965 vs 1969 citations.

- ⚠ (verbatim-quote not locally verifiable) — Leo 1991 block quote, `general-parsing.md:264`
  (`grep -n "improved from"`). The second clause "_both the time and space complexity will be
  improved from `O(n²)` to `O(n)`_" could **not** be confirmed verbatim: Leo has no local PDF,
  ScienceDirect/academia.edu returned HTTP 403, and the Utrecht/Semantic-Scholar pages exposed
  no abstract body. The first clause is confirmed by the paper's title and search hits; the
  "O(n²)→O(n)" wording is the form used across reliable secondary sources (Kegler's Marpa
  writings, Wikipedia-Earley), so it is very likely correct. One WebSearch AI-summary rendered
  it as "Ω(n·2^k) to O(n)" — treated as confabulation, not evidence of misquote. **Recommend:
  no change, but the survey could mark this quote's locator as `leo-uu` (abstract) explicitly,
  or downgrade to a paraphrase if a verbatim source can't be archived.**
