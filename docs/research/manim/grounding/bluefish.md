# Grounding: Bluefish (declarative relational diagramming)

Claim-by-claim source verification of [`bluefish.md`](../bluefish.md). The
deep-dive is grounded in the peer-reviewed paper (MIT Visualization Group copy +

**Verification note (this session).** The arXiv HTML (`arxiv.org/html/2307.00146v4`)
was re-fetched and the load-bearing quotes below were confirmed **verbatim**
(abstract, Cassowary removal, viscosity, local-propagation, performance figures,
bounding-box limitation, the Manim mention). **One discrepancy (D1):** the
deep-dive's compound-graph quote wording (_"Bluefish's relational scenegraph is an
instance of a `compound graph`: a data structure that maintains the hierarchical
information of traditional scenegraphs while also encoding adjacency relationships
between nodes."_) was **not** surfaced verbatim by the fetch; the arXiv text that
was returned reads instead _"…Bluefish extends a traditional tree-based scenegraph
to a compound graph that captures both hierarchical and adjacent relationships
between nodes."_ Same claim, different wording — likely a different section of the
paper than the one the fetch model returned. Flagged below (Q4 → ◯).
