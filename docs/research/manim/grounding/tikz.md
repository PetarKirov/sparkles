# Grounding: TikZ / PGF (TeX-native vector graphics)

> Not published research. Do not link to it from the survey pages.

Claim-by-claim source verification of [`tikz.md`](../tikz.md). The deep-dive is
grounded in the official `pgfmanual` (HTML edition at `tikz.dev`, a verbatim
rendering of the manual shipped in `pgf-tikz/pgf`), the CTAN package page, the
beamer manual, and the in-repo `LICENSE` — all public URLs. Version/license facts
pinned to **3.1.11a** (2025-08-29).
