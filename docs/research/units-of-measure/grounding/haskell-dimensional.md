## Discrepancies

- **#D1 ⚠ (minor, citation locator — 3 spots) — `haskell-dimensional.md:530`, `:590`,
  `:715`.** The "state of the art as far as units of measure in Haskell are concerned"
  quote and the LCSU payoff quote are both cited to "Gundry 2015 §7"; the paper has no §7
  (structure: §5 Related Work → §5.2 "The units Package" → §6 Conclusion with §6.1
  future work). Both quotes open/close §5.2.
  **Fix:** §7 → §5.2 in the contrast intro, the LCSU quote cite, and the Sources bullet.
  Fixed: yes (this pass).

- **#D2 ⚠ (minor, locator) — `haskell-dimensional.md:446`.** First documented error
  cited "L99–111"; the intro sentence starts at `Dimensional.hs:98` and the block ends at
  `:110` ("In other cases…" is `:112`).
  **Fix:** "L98–110". Fixed: yes (this pass).

- **#D3 ⚠ (minor, quote truncation) — `haskell-dimensional.md:477`.** The second
  documented error block (labelled L112–124) omitted the final two source lines
  (`In an equation for `x': …`), i.e. it actually reproduced only L112–122.
  **Fix:** the two missing lines appended, making the block verbatim for L112–124.
  Fixed: yes (this pass).

- **#D4 ⚠ (minor misquote) — `haskell-dimensional.md:573-574`.** Quote ended "…conclude
  that the quantity is 2 radians"; source `units-defs/Data/Dimensions/SI.hs:30-31` reads
  "…is 2 radians **or degrees**. To make plane angle safe to use, we define it as a
  fundamental dimension." Mid-sentence cut without ellipsis.
  **Fix:** "or degrees" restored inside the quote. Fixed: yes (this pass).

- **#D5 ⚠ (minor, mechanism) — `haskell-dimensional.md:236-238`.** "The `NRoot d x`
  family … simply has no equation when any division is inexact" — `NRoot`'s third
  equation always matches a concrete `'Dim`; the stuckness comes from `numtype-dk`'s
  partial type-level `/` (`N./`), which is what lacks a matching equation for inexact
  division. Net behavior (compile error) was stated correctly.
  **Fix:** reworded to attribute the stuckness to `numtype-dk`'s partial division.
  Fixed: yes (this pass).

- **#D6 ⚠ (minor, standards locator) — `haskell-dimensional.md:337-338` (+ trade-off
  table `:700`).** "prohibitions on double and stand-alone prefixes (§6.2.6…)" — in NIST
  SP 811, stand-alone prefixes are §6.2.6 ("Unacceptability of stand-alone prefixes") but
  double/compound prefixes are **§6.2.4** ("Unacceptability of compound prefixes"). The
  dimensional source cites only §6.2.6.
  **Fix:** "compound (§6.2.4) and stand-alone (§6.2.6, the section cited in the source)
  prefixes"; table cell now "NIST §6.2.4/§6.2.6 made structural". Fixed: yes (this pass).
